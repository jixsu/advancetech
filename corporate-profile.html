Corporate Profile

Here at Advancetech, lighting is our passion. Since 2007, Advancetech has been developing, engineering and manufacturing innovative LED solutions to compete in the growing market of sustainable lighting technology. Our high-performance LEDs and PCBAs are engineered to be energy-efficient and performance-oriented to provide products of the highest quality. Our vision is to provide high quality lighting while reducing energy consumption and the detrimental impacts on the environment seen in traditional lighting.

 

Advancetech specializes at delivering integrated product solutions from China and support to the North America industry clients. We offer manufacturing facilities and partners throughout Canada, the United States, and China, allowing Advancetech to provide its customers the flexibility to competitively achieve total cost solutions to their offshore-sourcing needs.

 

 

We help our clients to successfully buy industrial equipments and components, clean-tech equipment and high-tech products in China. Our services focus on:

 - Providing China supply market analysis and recommending the right suppliers for matching different clients’ outsourcing projects

 - Evaluating and qualifying new suppliers through factory on-site visits

 - Early involvement in project design to reduce the project designing lead time and the total cost of materials

 - Supply Development Program, develop partner suppliers to ensure they reach and meet our clients’ dynamic requirements

 - Sourcing the right parts/components from China to meet the clients’ requirements

 - Providing turn key solutions to reduce the Total cost Ownership of materials

 - Providing total supply chain management solutions to increase clients’ inventory turn over rate

 

 

Our clients are both industrial companies and small/medium-sized enterprises willing to develop their sourcing of parts and/or equipment from China. Thanks to our knowledge, expertise and proven process, we are able to:

 - Accelerate China Sourcing initiatives

 - Save unnecessary high project implementation costs

 - Provide local technical support and reduce the risks of products in designing, production process and assessment process

 - Provide advice on existing best practices, well adapted to the reality of the Chinese context
